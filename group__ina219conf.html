#define INA219_CONFIG_DEF   0x3DDF

Default sensor configuration for this library.

Sets the following options:

  • 32V measurement range
  • 320mV Shunt voltage range
  • 12 bit ADC resolution
  • Average over 8 samples
  • Sample continuously
